So You Want To Join The Team ...


Requirements

  • You must be a current student at HGP or VJM.
  • You should be interested in robotics/engineering/computer science. No prior experience required.
  • The student can not be currently failing a class.

First Steps

Read the About page to learn more about our team structure, and team meeting logistics.

Contact the coach to determine the date of the first after-school meeting you should attend.

Add yourself to the mailing list. (both parents and students)


Before the First Meeting

Check out some of the Team Resources to get an explanation of the current VEX challenge, and to begin your journey learning about the VEX systems and programming language.


Am I On The Team Now?

Students are considered members of the team once they have submitted the following materials:

  • A $150 check made out to Holy Ghost Preparatory School.
  • VEX Photo Release Form, signed by a parent or guardian. (second page of the PDF in the link)
  • A pair of safety goggles, to be kept in the lab. Students can take their own goggles home once the season is over.

You are view an up-to-date list of current members on our Teams page, under 'Member List.'
